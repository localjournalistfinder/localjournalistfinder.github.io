About

People trust local news more than any other kind—and for good reason. Local journalism keeps communities informed, connected, and engaged. In fact, 85% of Americans say local news is at least somewhat important to their community, with nearly half calling it extremely or very important.

San Francisco is home to a thriving local news scene, powered by curious, dedicated journalists. We built a tool to make it easier to find them.

The Local Journalist Finder helps you discover the people behind the bylines. Search by name, outlet, or topic to connect with the journalists covering what matters in your community.

Created by Enkhjin Munkhbayar and Hannah Woodworth at Stanford University, March 2024.
